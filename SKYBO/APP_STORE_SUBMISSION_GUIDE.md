- Phone: 1-800-633-2152 (US)

**Common Rejection Reasons to Avoid**

1. **Location Permission** - Make sure your location usage descriptions are clear
2. **Demo Account** - Provide working credentials with full access
3. **Privacy Policy** - Must be accessible without signing in
4. **Crash on Launch** - Test thoroughly before submission

8. **Release** once approved!
