# Privacy Policy for GeoGuard

**Last Updated: March 8, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to GeoGuard ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application GeoGuard (the "App").

**By using GeoGuard, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Full Name**: Used for identification within your organization
- **Company/Organization Name**: To create and manage your tenant organization
- **User Role**: Admin, Manager, or Field Personnel designation

### 1.2 Location Information

GeoGuard is a location tracking app designed for team safety. We collect:

- **Real-Time GPS Location**: Continuous location tracking when the app is in use or running in the background
- **Location History**: Stored location data for safety monitoring and compliance
- **Location Accuracy**: Precision of GPS coordinates
- **Speed and Heading**: Movement data for safety monitoring
- **Address Information**: Reverse-geocoded addresses from GPS coordinates

**Why We Need Location Access:**
- Monitor team member safety in real-time
- Provide emergency assistance through SOS features
- Generate safety check-in reports
- Track work site attendance
- Respond to incidents with accurate location data
- Enable geofencing capabilities

**Background Location:**
We access location data even when the app is closed or not in use to ensure continuous safety monitoring for field personnel.

### 1.3 Safety & Activity Data

- **Safety Check-Ins**: Time-stamped records of user safety status
- **Incident Reports**: User-submitted safety incidents, including descriptions and timestamps
- **SOS Alerts**: Emergency alert activations with location and timestamp
- **App Usage Data**: Feature usage patterns for improving functionality

### 1.4 Device Information

- **Device Type**: iOS device model
- **Operating System Version**: iOS version
- **App Version**: Current version of GeoGuard installed
- **Device Identifiers**: For push notifications and analytics

### 1.5 Automatically Collected Information

- **Log Data**: Timestamps, error logs, and app performance data
- **Crash Reports**: Technical data when the app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Analytics Data**: Aggregated usage statistics (via Firebase Analytics)

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

- **Firebase Cloud Firestore**: Google's secure, scalable database
- **Firebase Authentication**: Industry-standard user authentication
- **Google Cloud Platform**: Enterprise-grade infrastructure
- **Data Centers**: Located in secure, certified facilities

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement robust security measures including:

- End-to-end encryption for data transmission (TLS/SSL)
- Encrypted data storage at rest
- Role-based access controls
- Tenant isolation (your organization's data is separated from others)
- Regular security audits and updates
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Firewall and intrusion detection systems

### 3.3 Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Location Data**: Retained for 90 days (configurable by organization admins)
- **Safety Records**: Retained for regulatory compliance periods (typically 1-7 years)
- **Account Deletion**: Data deleted within 30 days of account closure (except where legally required to retain)

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data**: Request a copy of all data we have about you
- **Update Information**: Modify your profile information in the app
- **Delete Account**: Request account deletion (contact support)
- **Export Data**: Request a portable copy of your data
- **Opt-Out of Analytics**: Disable analytics tracking (where applicable)

### 5.2 Location Permissions

You can control location access through:

- **iOS Settings**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → GeoGuard
- **Permission Levels**: 
  - "Always": Required for background tracking (field personnel)
  - "While Using": Limited functionality
  - "Never": Core features will not work

**Important**: Disabling location access will prevent core safety features from functioning.

### 5.3 Push Notifications

Control notification settings:
- **iOS Settings**: Settings → Notifications → GeoGuard
- **In-App**: Configure alert preferences in settings

### 5.4 Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of:
- Promotional emails (using unsubscribe link)
- Non-essential push notifications
- Marketing communications

**Note**: You cannot opt-out of critical safety alerts or service notifications.
